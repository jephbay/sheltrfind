New to shelterFind? Don’t worry, this guide is here to help in your quest to add a new facility. The steps are indicated below;

  • In the home page where you have the list of facilities, tap on “Profile” - the last menu to the right in the menus at the bottom of your screen.
  • Tapping on “Profile” opens a list of options available for the profile menu. Look for “Lift a facility” (The first item) in the options. This option is available if you have never added a facility in this app, but for a user who has a facility and would like to add another or edit the existing facility, the “Lift a facility” option would be replaced with the “Manage” option.
  • After selecting the “Lift a facility” option, the next screen displays the category of facilities you can add (Hostel, Hotel, Homes/Apartments, House). Only one category can be selected at a time. Select a category and tap on the “Next” button to go to the next screen.
  • The set of options that would be displayed to you depends on the category of facilities you choose. The next set of steps and instructions are self-explanatory. Follow them carefully to successfully complete your quest for adding a new facility.

Note: Some steps are compulsory, while others can be skipped. You can always stop at any step if you don’t have the information at the moment and continue anytime later.
